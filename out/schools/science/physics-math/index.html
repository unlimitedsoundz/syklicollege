Department of Applied Physics & Mathematics

The Department of Applied Physics & Mathematics at SYKLI College provides the scientific and analytical foundation that underpins modern technology, engineering, and data-driven innovation.

Study Programs

Bachelor's in Applied Physics

This program offers a deep dive into the physical laws that govern technological progress. From quantum mechanics to thermodynamics, students apply physics to solve engineering challenges. The curriculum includes hands-on laboratory work and research projects in fields like nanotechnology and renewable energy.

Master's in Mathematics and Systems Analysis

This MSc offers advanced training in mathematical modeling and systems analysis. Students master complex optimization techniques and stochastic modeling, applicable to sectors like logistics, finance, and engineering. The program is research-intensive, preparing graduates for PhD studies or high-level industrial research roles.

Bachelor's in Mathematics and Systems Analysis

A rigorous program combining advanced mathematics with systems engineering principles. Students learn to model complex processes in nature, society, and engineering. The curriculum covers optimization, stochastic processes, and mathematical modeling, providing the tools to solve the most difficult technical problems of the 21st century.
